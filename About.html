The Manaia is a mythical creature from the Maori Culture. This creature is represented by the head of a bird, the body of a snake and the tail of a whale and symbolises the balance between sky, earth and sea. The Manaia has inspired and influenced me in many aspects of my life and in my designs. Thus, Manaia Design was born and became my philosophy.

My name is Terry, I am a Mauritian born Graphic Designer. I studied and lived in Australia for three years where I also graduated with a Diploma in Graphic Arts. I have acquired work experience in New Zealand, Australia, Mauritius and Germany. I am currently based in Hamburg, Germany so feel free to hit me up if you have ideas, suggestions or a project you would like to collaborate on.
